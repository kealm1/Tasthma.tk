About Us

We are a multicultural team of post graduate IT students at Monash university, Melbourne. Our team is composed of an expert coder, a software testing specialist, a security specialist and a business analyst. We are currently completing our final semester at Monash. Web application TAsthma is the product of our final project at Monash - Thunderstorm Asthma.

We support the Victoria's Climate Change Adaptation Plan (2017 - 2020), which is focused on supporting communities to be healthy, connected and resilient, no matter how the climate changes. We are passionate about making a difference to the quality of life of our fellow Victorians.
